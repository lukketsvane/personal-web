# Korall Projects: Pioneering Future Designs

Founded on the innovative spirit and pioneering work of its co-founders, including my own journey as CEO from November 2019 to January 2021, Korall Projects AS has carved its niche in the projects world. Located in the heart of Bergen, Norway, our startup initially known as Coral Solutions, has evolved into a leader in design for additive manufacturing, focusing on the integration of optimization algorithms and technologies like TPMS/lattice structures into automated design workflows.

<img src="https://i.ibb.co/Korall-Projects-Innovation" alt="Korall Projects Innovation" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto', marginBottom: '20px' }} />

## Our Vision

Our mission at Korall Projects is to revolutionize the design process, making it greener, more efficient, and tailored to the needs of the future. As part of StartupLab Bergen and a member of the GCE Ocean Technology Cluster, we are at the forefront of employing high-end product optimization technologies to meet the demands of various industries.

## Core Competencies

We excel in a range of services aimed at pushing the boundaries of traditional and additive manufacturing:

- **Design Optimization**: Reducing material waste and improving part efficiency.
- **Automated Product Design Workflow**: Customizing and 3D printing industrial parts on demand.
- **Part Consolidation**: Merging multiple components into a single, efficient design.
- **Topology and Generative Design**: Utilizing AI and mathematical models for optimal part design.
- **Digitalization and 3D Printing**: Assisting companies in adopting digital warehouses and additive manufacturing technologies.

## Our Journey in Numbers

- **Market Presence**: Over 1,000 wheel types with more than 126,000 units in 36 installations.
- **Innovation at Scale**: Our automated design process allows for unparalleled customization and efficiency.

## Reflecting on Leadership

During my tenure as CEO, I was deeply involved in developing novel projects solutions, navigating company management challenges, and advancing the field of additive manufacturing. Although I have moved on from day-to-day operations, my contributions to Korall Projects's foundation continue to inspire its path forward.

# DongJin Tableware - Elevating Dining Experiences Through Design

At DongJin Tableware (Yangxin) Co., Ltd, I served as an Executive Designer, leading a dynamic team in creating innovative tableware designs. Our work combined aesthetic appeal with functional excellence, setting new standards in the industry.

<img src="https://i.ibb.co/DongJin-Logo" alt="DongJin Logo" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto', marginBottom: '20px' }} />

## Position Overview

**Title**: Executive Designer  
**Company**: DongJin Tableware (Yangxin) Co., Ltd  
**Duration**: Sep 2016 - Dec 2016 (4 months)  
**Location**: Yangxin, Shandong, China  

## Roles and Responsibilities

- **Team Leadership**: Navigated a team of four people responsible for marketing, design, and production.
- **Training**: Taught workers in product photo techniques and design theory.
- **Graphic Design & Photography**: Created presentations and catalogs.
- **3D Modeling**: Engaged in 3D modeling tasks for production purposes.

## Software Skills

- Autodesk Fusion 360
- Rhino
- InDesign
- Photoshop / Lightroom
- 3ds Max
- V-ray
- Keyshot

## Contributions

- Created effective marketing materials through graphic design and photography.
- Trained workers, elevating the overall quality of product photos and design.
